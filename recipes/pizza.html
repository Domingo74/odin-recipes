This recipe is one of the best, it can serve up to 16 servingns and yield 2 14-inch pizzas!

It's easy to do, and you can please your friends to a delicious meal!

Prep time: 30 mins Cook time: 10 mins Additional time: 16 hrs 5 mins

Total time: 16 hrs 45 mins Servings: 16 Yield: 2 14-inch pizzas

Igredients

    Dough:

  • ¼ cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

    • Toppings:

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn
